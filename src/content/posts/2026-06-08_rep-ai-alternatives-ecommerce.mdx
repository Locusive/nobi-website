## Is Rep AI still the right tool for your ecommerce team?

[Rep AI](https://hellorep.ai) is the most purpose-built proactive-chat platform for Shopify on the market. Flow Studio lets non-engineers design deterministic conversation flows per campaign (Black Friday promos, product launches, abandoned-cart rescues) without engineering support. A live-agent drop-in lets a human join an active AI session mid-thread without forcing a binary handoff. An in-chat Post-Order Tasks Agent handles cancellations and tracking lookups. Drop-off-reason analytics tell you why shoppers bailed, not just that they did. Those are real, differentiated capabilities.

The reason most teams start evaluating alternatives is that Rep AI is chat-only. It doesn't rank search results, manage category pages, or handle agent-side ticket workflows. Teams whose conversion gap is search abandonment or null results will hit its ceiling quickly.

Pricing also creates friction at scale. The Pay As You Grow entry tier runs $12 per 1,000 visitors with a 500-product catalog cap - a common growth blocker. Pro tiers at around 125,000 monthly sessions run $280 to $740 per month.

If your gap is site search, quiz-led discovery, agent-side ticket workflows, or a human-staffed widget with AI backup, one of the alternatives below is a more direct fit.

## How did we evaluate these Rep AI alternatives?

We evaluated each option on three things: job fit (does it directly address the conversion problem your team actually has?), catalog grounding (are answers based on live product and policy data, or static training that can go stale?), and pricing transparency (can you model the monthly bill before a demo?). Nobi is one of the tools in this list. We built it, and we've tried to describe it alongside the others honestly, including where a competitor is the better pick.

Catalog grounding matters more than it looks on a feature sheet. A tool that answers from infrequently updated training data will eventually tell a shopper a product is in stock when it isn't, or quote a return policy that changed six months ago. That's the kind of mistake that loses the customer for good - not just the sale.

We excluded tools whose primary function is email-first lead nurture rather than on-site conversion, and tools with uncertain standalone roadmaps following recent acquisitions. Every tool here publishes a starting price; we've used those published rates throughout.

## 2. Octane AI

[Octane AI](https://octaneai.com) is a quiz-led product discovery platform for Shopify, and the quiz itself is the discovery experience. Shoppers work through five to ten diagnostic questions (fit, use case, occasion, budget) and the platform maps their answers to product attributes to surface a specific SKU. For a marketing team sitting on a wide catalog where shoppers often don't know what they want, that structure is the appeal. Octane AI runs natively on Shopify, ships pre-built templates for fit finders and routine-style flows, and connects quiz completions directly to Klaviyo as email-segment attributes.

**Best for:** Ecommerce teams whose main path to product discovery is a diagnostic quiz (fit, use case, occasion, budget) where shoppers need guided questions to narrow a wide catalog to the right SKU.

**Pricing:** Public tiered Shopify-app pricing. Basic $50/month (400 credits), Plus $200/month (2,200 credits), Enterprise & Unlimited starts at $500+/month. Octane defines a credit as one quiz completion ("1 credit = 1 quiz engagement when someone completes your quiz"). AI features (Smart Products, Smart Copy, Image Analyzer) consume fractional extra credits per interaction.

**Pros:**
- Quiz builder with deep Shopify product-attribute mapping, so quiz answers resolve to real SKUs rather than generic category pages
- Native Klaviyo integration captures quiz answers as email-segment attributes, so a quiz completion flows straight into lifecycle email without a separate integration

**Cons:**
- Quiz-centric, less about open-ended conversation
- It's strongest in beauty and CPG; less suited for categories where open-ended search or broad browsing is the primary discovery path.

**Verdict:** Pick Octane AI when a guided quiz is genuinely the right discovery path for your shoppers; skip it when customers are typing open-ended questions or already know what they want and just need to find it on site.

## 3. Gorgias

[Gorgias](https://gorgias.com) is an ecommerce helpdesk built around agent-side workflow: tickets, routing, macros, returns, and refund processing. Shopify order context sits inside every ticket, so when a shopper emails about a missing package or a return, the agent sees the order, customer history, and product details in one place - and can cancel, refund, or modify without switching tools. For ecommerce teams where post-purchase ticket volume is the real bottleneck (WISMO, exchanges, return requests), it's a more purpose-built pick than a chat-first tool.

**Best for:** DTC and ecommerce teams on Shopify whose main bottleneck is post-purchase ticket volume (WISMO, returns, exchanges) and who want email, chat, and social support in one ecommerce-aware inbox.

**Pricing:** Tiered by ticket volume. Base starts low; AI add-on extra.

**Pros:**
- Mature automation: macros, routing rules, SLA tracking, and multi-brand tenant support for teams running multiple labels off one stack
- Structured returns and refund workflows that chat-only tools don't replicate
- Multi-channel inbox covering email, live chat, and social

**Cons:**
- Helpdesk-first; requires committing to a ticketing system
- AI features available on higher tiers only
- No product discovery or search capability

**Verdict:** Pick Gorgias when post-purchase ticket volume (WISMO, returns, exchanges) is the core problem you're solving; skip it when you need a pre-purchase AI assistant driving product discovery and CVR.

## 4. Tidio

[Tidio](https://tidio.com) is a live-chat platform built for Shopify merchants, with Lyro AI layered on top to handle first-touch shopper questions when no agent is available. The product is built around a staffed team first. The agent inbox, keyboard shortcuts, and Shopify order context in the ticket view are all designed for operators who live in that view all day. Lyro fills the gaps: after-hours coverage, standard WISMO queries, and first-touch questions that don't need a specialist on the line. AI and human modes work alongside each other rather than competing, so the handoff feels native rather than a hard cutover. That structure fits a marketing manager running a lean support team who needs AI to back the humans up, not replace them.

**Best for:** Ecommerce brands whose priority is a staffed 24/7 chat widget with humans owning complex product and policy questions, and AI covering after-hours and first-touch volume.

**Pricing:** Free tier available for piloting the chat widget. Paid plans start at $24.17/mo (Starter, 100 conversations) and $49.17/mo (Growth, 250 conversations). Lyro AI is a separate add-on from $32.50/mo for 50 AI conversations. Billed annually; monthly pricing is slightly higher.

**Pros:**
- Affordable free tier lets teams get live chat running before committing budget, with Lyro as a pay-when-needed upgrade

**Cons:**
- Lighter on product discovery and semantic search
- Lyro AI responder is first-touch oriented, not a deep conversational agent

**Verdict:** Pick Tidio when the job is a staffed 24/7 chat widget with AI as a safety net for standard questions; skip it if AI-led product discovery or catalog-grounded pre-purchase Q&A is the primary conversion lever you are trying to move.

## Which Rep AI alternative fits your ecommerce stack?

The right pick depends on where you are losing revenue today. Each tool in this list does a different job, and the most common mistake is evaluating by feature count rather than matching the tool to the actual conversion gap.

**Nobi** is the most direct fix when site search is the bottleneck: shoppers hitting null results, dead ends, or a vocabulary mismatch between what they type and how the catalog is tagged. Search and catalog-grounded Q&A come in one product from $25/month.

**Rep AI** is the right pick when scripted campaign flows are the priority: no-code Black Friday conversation paths, product-launch walk-throughs, or abandoned-cart rescue sequences built without engineering tickets.

**Octane AI** fits when quiz-led discovery is genuinely how shoppers narrow a wide catalog. Guided questions around fit, use case, or budget map to a specific SKU. At $50/month on the Basic plan, it's the most direct tool for teams whose primary discovery path is a diagnostic quiz rather than open-ended search.

**Gorgias** covers the post-purchase ticket problem in a way chat-first tools do not. WISMO volume, exchange and refund processing, multi-channel ticket routing - agent-side workflow is what it is built for.

**Tidio** is the most affordable starting point when a human-staffed 24/7 chat widget is the priority: agents owning complex questions, Lyro AI covering first touch and after-hours. The free base tier lets you get the widget running before committing budget.

Teams whose gaps span both pre-purchase discovery and post-purchase ticket volume will likely need separate tools to cover both jobs. Gorgias handles agent-side ticket workflows that none of the chat-first tools in this list cover. Model the cost of each against the revenue impact of the gap before committing.

## Frequently asked questions

**Does Nobi replace Rep AI entirely?**

Only if your primary use case is search and pre-purchase Q&A. Nobi covers product discovery and catalog-grounded shopper answers; it doesn't replicate Rep AI's Flow Studio no-code conversation builder, live-agent drop-in, or in-chat post-purchase task execution.

**Can these tools run alongside each other?**

Yes. Each tool in this list covers a different job. Gorgias handles post-purchase ticket workflows independently; the pre-purchase discovery tools handle search and chat. Tools with no functional overlap can run on the same stack without conflicts.

**Which tool is fastest to get running?**

Tidio and Nobi both have quick installs; the variable is how long knowledge base setup takes. Rep AI and Gorgias both require more upfront work; building Flow Studio conversation trees and configuring ticket routing rules each take time.

**Which tool best handles WISMO?**

Gorgias handles order status questions as part of its helpdesk: agents see Shopify order context in every ticket and can act on it directly. Nobi's assistant can answer order status questions if that information is in its knowledge base, but it doesn't pull live transactional order data by default.

**Is Rep AI only for Shopify?**

Rep AI is built specifically for Shopify. Nobi, Octane AI, Gorgias, and Tidio all have strong Shopify integrations, with varying support for WooCommerce and headless stacks.
