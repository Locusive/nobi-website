## What is voice AI for car dealerships?

Voice AI for car dealerships is software that uses AI agents to handle phone calls - inbound and outbound - on behalf of the sales or service department. The agent answers questions about inventory, qualifies a lead, books a test drive or service appointment, and hands off to a human when it can't. Most platforms in this category extend beyond voice into SMS, email, and web chat, so "voice AI" usually means one channel inside a broader conversational AI tool rather than a phone-only product.

The common jobs are inbound call answering, lead qualification, service appointment booking, and outbound follow-up. To do those well, the agent has to read from the dealership's CRM and DMS to look up live inventory and write appointments back into the service scheduler.

[Impel](https://impel.ai), [Matador AI](https://matador.ai), and [Toma](https://toma.com) all bundle phone with SMS, email, and chat - voice is rarely sold standalone in this category. A separate category of tools handles the website side: [on-site shopper questions](https://nobi.ai/blog/ai-shopping-assistant-ecommerce) answered from the dealership's own pages (FAQ, financing, policies), rather than phone calls.

## Frequently asked questions

**Does voice AI replace the BDC?** Not in practice. Tools like [Impel](https://impel.ai), [Matador AI](https://matador.ai), and [Toma](https://toma.com) are typically deployed to handle first response, after-hours overflow, and high-volume repetitive calls (service appointment booking, recall checks, basic status questions). The BDC stays in place for upset callers, complex deals, and any conversation that needs the in-person context only a human picks up. Toma is explicit that its voice agents aren't suitable for upset callers or sensitive issues - that's the right framing for any of these vendors.

**How does pricing usually work?** Voice AI for dealerships is almost always quote-based per rooftop, with no public starting price. Impel, Matador AI, and Toma all gate pricing behind a sales conversation. Nobi publishes its numbers on the web side: $25/month base, including 2,500 searches and 250 conversational messages, with $0.01 per additional search and $0.10 per additional message.

**What about TCPA and SMS consent?** Matador AI publishes the most explicit TCPA tooling of the four. Verify the consent and opt-out flow with any vendor before sending outbound texts.

**Do I need both voice AI and web AI?** Most dealerships end up running both because the jobs are different. Voice AI (Impel, Matador AI, Toma) handles the phone - inbound calls, after-hours, service scheduling. Web AI (Nobi) handles the website - shopper questions on financing FAQs, trade-in basics, policy pages, and warranty coverage. One doesn't replace the other.
