## What is Answer engine optimization (AEO)?

Where traditional SEO targets ranked links in a results page, AEO targets the direct answers that AI systems synthesize and surface at the top of a response. Content that succeeds at AEO is typically well-structured, factually precise, and organized around the specific questions real users ask. Because AI answer engines pull from pages they can parse and trust, AEO also overlaps with technical markup, schema, and clear authorship signals.

## How does answer engine optimization work?

- Write content in a question-and-answer format so AI systems can match a user's query to a specific passage
- Use structured data markup (FAQ schema, HowTo schema, Product schema) to make page sections machine-readable
- Keep answers concise and factual - one clear answer per question, with supporting detail following
- Earn citations by building topical authority and ensuring facts are accurate and consistent across the site

## Why does it matter?

AI answer engines are increasingly the first place shoppers and car buyers look for product information, comparisons, and how-to guidance - often before they visit a brand's website at all. Operators who structure their content for AEO can appear in those AI-generated answers, reaching buyers at the moment of decision. Ignoring AEO means competitors' pages - or generic summaries - fill that space instead.

[Nobi](https://dashboard.nobi.ai)'s product-page assistant captures and outputs real shopper Q&A as structured data, giving those pages the question-and-answer format and machine-readable signals that make them eligible to be cited by AI answer engines.

## Frequently asked questions

**Is AEO the same as SEO?**
They overlap but are not the same. SEO focuses on ranking in traditional link-based results, while AEO focuses on getting content extracted and cited by AI systems that generate direct answers. Good AEO tends to support SEO, but the tactics - especially around structured data and question formatting - go further.

**What types of content perform best in AI answer engines?**
Content that directly answers a specific question, uses clear and factual language, and is marked up with structured schema tends to perform best. FAQ pages, product detail pages with Q&A sections, and how-to guides are strong formats.

**How do I know if my pages are being cited by AI answer engines?**
You can test manually by asking relevant questions in tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google AI Overviews and checking whether your site is referenced. Some SEO platforms are also beginning to track AI citation share as a separate metric alongside traditional rank tracking.
