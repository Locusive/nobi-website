## What is a shopping bot, and what does it actually do on an ecommerce site?

A shopping bot is software that helps a visitor find, evaluate, or buy products on your store through four surfaces: [site search](https://nobi.ai/blog/ai-vs-traditional-site-search), conversational chat, a guided quiz, or product-detail-page Q&A. Each surface solves a different conversion problem, so picking the right tool starts with naming which job is your real gap.

Nobi sits in the search-plus-assistant lane, combining site search, conversational AI, and automatic shopper Q&A at $25/month base (2,500 searches and 250 messages, with $0.01 and $0.10 overages after that). Strong when search relevance and pre-purchase questions both leak conversions; weaker if you need quiz-led discovery.

[Rep AI](https://www.hellorep.ai/) runs proactive, scripted sales chat with behavioral AI that intervenes mid-session through flow-builder campaigns. Strong when browsing abandonment is the bottleneck; weaker for open-ended catalog Q&A.

[Tidio](https://www.tidio.com/) pairs live human chat with its Lyro AI agent at $0.50 per conversation, with 50 free to start. Strong when human-plus-AI support coverage is the priority; weaker as a primary discovery surface.

[Octane AI](https://www.octaneai.com/) owns the quiz-led product finder job on Shopify, from $50/month. Pick it when your hero discovery surface is a quiz, not search.

The buying question isn't "which is best" - it's which job is your actual conversion gap.

## 1. Nobi

Nobi is an [AI shopping platform](https://nobi.ai/blog/ai-shopping-assistant-ecommerce) that runs site search, a conversational assistant, and automatic shopper Q&A on a single product, so the same engine ranking results on the search page also answers questions in chat and on PDPs. For a marketing manager whose conversion gap is shoppers landing, hunting, and bouncing before they find what they need, that means one install covers the on-site discovery surface end-to-end - no stitching a search vendor to a chat vendor to a Q&A vendor and praying the three stay in sync. **Verdict:** Pick Nobi when on-site search and conversational discovery are the conversion gap; look elsewhere if your shoppers live on SMS or WhatsApp, or if you need self-serve post-purchase task execution inside the chat.

## 2. Rep AI

[Rep AI](https://hellorep.ai) is the most mature purpose-built proactive-chat tool for Shopify, and the standout feature for a marketing manager is Flow Studio - a visual no-code builder that lets non-engineers design deterministic conversation flows. That means a Black Friday flow, a product-launch walk-through, or an abandoned-cart rescue ships without an engineering ticket and without waiting on a sprint. Pair that with live-agent drop-in (a human can join an active AI session while the AI keeps handling it, instead of forcing a binary handoff) and an in-chat agent that processes cancellations, returns, and tracking lookups, and Rep AI fits teams whose campaign calendar is the gating factor on conversational sales chat.

**Best for:** Marketing ops and CX teams who want to script campaign-specific conversation flows without engineering, or who need a human agent to jump into an active AI session mid-thread.

**Pricing:** $12 per 1,000 visitors on the Pay As You Grow tier (unlimited sessions, 500-product catalog cap). At ~125K monthly sessions, Pro tiers run $280/mo (Support), $616/mo (Sales), $740/mo (Bundle). Enterprise custom above ~500K sessions. 30-day free trial.

**Pros:**
- Flow Studio visual builder lets non-engineers design deterministic flows for promos, product launches, and abandoned-cart rescue
- Live-agent drop-in is a true hybrid - a human can join an AI session while the AI keeps handling it, rather than a binary handoff
- Post-Order Tasks Agent lets shoppers self-serve cancellations, returns, and tracking lookups inside the chat widget
- Drop-off-reasons analytics answer why shoppers abandoned, not just that they did

**Cons:**
- Sales-chat-only - no site search, so discovery outside the chat widget still needs a separate tool
- Personalization is scoped to the chat session; ranking and merchandising anywhere outside the chat widget aren't part of what Rep AI does

**Verdict:** Pick Rep AI when you need a no-code flow builder, an AI+human hybrid chat mode, or in-chat post-purchase task execution; pair it with a search tool if on-site discovery is also a priority.

## 3. Tidio

[Tidio](https://tidio.com) is a live-chat platform built for Shopify merchants, with Lyro layered on top as the AI responder that handles first-touch shopper questions when human agents are offline. The paradigm matters here: Tidio assumes humans still own the conversation and AI fills the gaps, not the other way around. For a marketing manager whose support team already staffs a chat widget and whose real problem is after-hours coverage, that framing maps cleanly to how the tool behaves day-to-day. The agent inbox, keyboard shortcuts, and Shopify order context inside the ticket view are built for human operators first. Lyro is positioned as the safety net that catches questions when those operators are asleep or at capacity.

**Best for:** Brands whose priority is a staffed 24/7 chat widget with humans in the loop and AI covering after-hours and first-touch questions.

**Pricing:** Lyro AI billed at $0.50 per resolved conversation, with 50 free conversations to start. Available as an add-on to any Tidio plan or bundled into the Plus plan, with unlimited Lyro replies once metered.

**Pros:**
- Well-designed agent inbox with keyboard shortcuts and Shopify order context surfaced inline, which matters when your team lives in the ticket view
- AI and human modes are complementary rather than competing paradigms, so the handoff feels native rather than a binary cutover
- Broad channel support beyond web chat (Messenger, Instagram, email) for brands whose shoppers are genuinely multi-channel
- Lowest-friction free pilot in this list - 50 Lyro conversations at no cost before any commitment

**Cons:**
- Lyro is first-touch oriented rather than a deep conversational discovery agent; shoppers hit the ceiling on complex product questions quickly
- Weaker on semantic product discovery than tools whose primary surface is search, so it's not the right pick if browse-and-find is the conversion gap

**Verdict:** Pick Tidio when the job is a staffed 24/7 live-chat widget with AI as a safety net, not when AI-led product discovery is the main conversion lever.

## 4. Octane AI

[Octane AI](https://octaneai.com) is a quiz-led product finder built primarily for Shopify, and the quiz is the hero surface, not open-ended chat or site search. For a marketing manager in beauty, skincare, or supplements, that focus is the point: shoppers answer five to ten diagnostic questions about skin type, goals, or routine, and the platform maps answers to product attributes in the Shopify catalog to recommend a regimen. Octane positions itself as the leading product quiz platform for Shopify and ships pre-built templates for skincare routines, supplement regimens, and fit finders. The Klaviyo handoff is the marketing payoff - quiz answers become email-segment attributes, so quiz completion flows straight into lifecycle campaigns instead of dying as one-off recommendations.

**Best for:** Beauty, skincare, and CPG brands whose primary discovery surface is a diagnostic or routine-builder quiz funnel.

**Pricing:** Basic at $50/month (400 credits), Plus at $200/month (2,200 credits), Enterprise & Unlimited at $500+/month.

**Pros:**
- Quiz builder with deep Shopify product-attribute mapping, so recommendations map to real SKUs and attributes
- Pre-built templates for common vertical use cases - skincare routines, supplement regimens, fit finders - which shortens time-to-launch
- Native Klaviyo integration captures quiz answers as email-segment attributes, so quiz completion flows straight into lifecycle email
- Lower engineering overhead than building quiz logic in-house on top of a headless storefront

**Cons:**
- Quiz-centric, so it's less effective for shoppers who prefer open-ended search or conversational browsing over a scripted question tree
- Vertical fit narrows outside beauty and CPG categories, where scripted diagnostic questions don't map cleanly to catalog attributes

**Verdict:** Pick Octane AI if a quiz is genuinely the right discovery metaphor for your category; skip it if your catalog is too varied or technical for a scripted question tree to cover.

## How should a marketing manager pick the right shopping bot?

Pick by the job that's actually broken in the funnel, not by feature checklist. Each of the four tools above owns a different lane, and the buyer-regret stories come from mismatched pairings.

If shoppers land and bounce because they can't find products on search results or PDPs, the broken job is search and conversational discovery. Nobi runs site search, chat, and automatic shopper Q&A on one platform at $25/month base (2,500 searches and 250 messages included), then $0.01 per additional search and $0.10 per additional message. [Lucchese](/customers/lucchese) drove $1M+ in incremental revenue in year one running Nobi search plus a cart and PDP assistant. Limit worth naming: Nobi is web-chat only and doesn't ship a quiz-led product finder, so brands whose hero discovery surface is a diagnostic quiz should pick differently.

If marketing ops needs to ship campaign-specific scripted chat flows without filing engineering tickets, the broken job is flow authoring. [Rep AI](https://hellorep.ai)'s Flow Studio, AI+human hybrid mode, and Post-Order Tasks Agent are purpose-built for that workflow. Right pick when shipping a Black Friday or product-launch flow on deadline is the bottleneck; weaker pick when open-ended catalog search is.

If the priority is staffing a 24/7 chat widget with humans plus AI for after-hours coverage, [Tidio](https://tidio.com)'s human-first inbox with Lyro layered on top is the paradigm. Right pick when agent staffing is the constraint; weaker pick when no live-chat operation exists yet.

If the category sells through a diagnostic quiz - skin type, supplement stack, fit finder - [Octane AI](https://octaneai.com) plus its Klaviyo segment handoff is the right metaphor, starting at $50/month. Right pick for quiz-first discovery; weaker for general site search or open-ended chat.

Two-tool stacks are common. A discovery-heavy store might run Nobi for on-site search and Q&A and Rep AI for scripted promotional flows. Pick by the bottleneck, not the brochure.

## Frequently asked questions

A few questions come up on every shopping bot evaluation: what the category actually means, whether engineering has to be involved, how pricing scales, and how the bot fits with the helpdesk you already run.

**What's the difference between a shopping bot and a generic site chat widget?** A generic chat widget is a messaging surface - it routes whatever the shopper types to a human, a canned reply, or a basic FAQ matcher. A shopping bot is grounded in your product catalog and order data, so it can answer "do you have this in a size 9" or "where's my order" with real answers, and it can recommend products instead of just collecting the question.

**Does installing one on Shopify need engineering?** Most options on this list (Rep AI, Tidio, Octane AI) install as Shopify apps - one-click from the App Store, theme blocks for placement. Nobi takes a different path: two small JavaScript snippets dropped into your theme, which is why it also runs on BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and Salesforce Commerce Cloud. Either way, no custom backend work.

**How do they price?** Models vary widely - flat monthly tiers, per-resolution fees, per-session pricing, and usage-based metering all show up in this category. Nobi prices usage-based: $25/month base (2,500 searches and 250 conversational messages), then $0.01 per additional search and $0.10 per additional message. No revenue share, no per-seat fees.

**Does it replace the helpdesk?** No. A shopping bot deflects shopper questions before they become tickets; a helpdesk like Gorgias or Zendesk handles the tickets that still come through, plus returns, refunds, and agent workflow. Run them together.

**How long until you see lift?** Implementation is days, not months, for the tools listed here. Measurable lift in conversion or ticket deflection usually shows up inside the first 30 days of traffic.
