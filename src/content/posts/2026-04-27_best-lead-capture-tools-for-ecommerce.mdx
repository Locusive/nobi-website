## What counts as a lead capture tool for an ecommerce brand?

A lead capture tool turns anonymous site traffic into a contact you can market to later, usually an email address or phone number. The category splits into four mechanics: popups and banners that interrupt the page, quiz funnels that trade product recommendations for an email, exit-intent and behavioral overlays, and conversational surfaces that ask for an email mid-discovery. Each captures a different slice of intent and fits a different on-site moment.

Nobi sits in a different lane: [AI site search](https://nobi.ai/blog/ai-vs-traditional-site-search), a shopping assistant that answers product questions in plain language, and automatic shopper Q&A on shipping, sizing, returns, and policy. $25/month base includes 2,500 searches and 250 conversational messages, with $0.01 per additional search and $0.10 per additional message. Strong when better on-site discovery would move conversion more than another email capture surface; not a popup or quiz tool, so brands whose primary need is email list growth will still want one of the three below.

[Privy](https://www.privy.com) is the default Shopify popup tool: email and SMS capture starting at $30/month with 250 SMS credits included. Strong for fast popup deployment on Shopify with email and SMS in the same tool; weaker once you need advanced features, where the next tier jumps to $199/month.

[Justuno](https://www.justuno.com) covers the same popup-and-banner lane with visitor-profile targeting and a founder-led onboarding process. Pick it when precise segment rules behind the overlay are the priority.

[Octane AI](https://www.octaneai.com) captures the email as the byproduct of a product-recommendation quiz, from $50/month on the Basic plan. Right pick when a quiz is the hero discovery surface.

## 2. Privy

[Privy](https://privy.com) is the default reach for Shopify merchants who want a popup builder, an email and SMS sender, and the simplest possible install all in one place. For a marketing manager, the appeal is speed: install the app, pick a welcome popup template, ship a coupon-for-email offer the same afternoon. The library covers the classic on-site capture mechanics - welcome overlays, exit-intent, scroll-triggered, cart abandonment - and higher tiers bundle email and SMS sending so the captured contact has somewhere to go without a second tool. It is a single-purpose product, focused tightly on the popup-driven capture motion.

**Best for:** Shopify brands whose lead capture program is built around classic popup mechanics - welcome, exit-intent, scroll, and cart-abandonment overlays.

**Pricing:** Free starter plan with limited contacts; paid plans start at $30/month and scale with mailable contacts and SMS volume (250 free SMS credits included at the $30 tier). Advanced features sit at the $199/month tier.

**Pros:**
- Deep popup library covering welcome, exit-intent, scroll-triggered, and cart-abandonment overlays out of the box
- Shopify-native install with a strong app store presence and a fast time to first popup
- Bundles email and SMS sending in higher tiers, so capture and send live in one tool
- Integrates with Klaviyo and BigCommerce when you outgrow the built-in sender

**Cons:**
- Steep jump from the $24 popup-and-displays tier to $199/month for advanced features, so growing capture sophistication can move pricing fast
- The built-in email and SMS sender is lighter on segmentation than a dedicated ESP, so most teams move to Klaviyo as the list grows

**Verdict:** Pick Privy when a fast-install Shopify popup tool with email and SMS in the same place is what you want; budget for the jump to the $199/month tier if you need advanced features down the line.

## 3. Justuno

[Justuno](https://justuno.com) is a conversion optimization platform built around popups, banners, overlays, and embedded forms, with a rule-based targeting engine sitting underneath all of it. The pitch to a marketing manager is granular control: who sees what offer, when, based on geo, behavior, traffic source, cart value, and dozens of other conditions. If you've outgrown a single welcome popup and want different offers firing for first-time visitors from paid social versus returning visitors with items in cart, this is the lane Justuno occupies. Built-in A/B testing lets you actually prove which rule combinations move the needle rather than guessing. The trade-off is depth: the rules engine rewards teams willing to invest time building targeting logic.

**Best for:** Brands running a sophisticated capture program across multiple surfaces who need rule-based segmentation, not just a single welcome popup.

**Pricing:** Free plan under traffic limits; paid plans start around $39/month and scale with monthly visitors. Modular billing means total cost depends on which campaigns and integrations you add.

**Pros:**
- Targeting rules cover geo, behavior, traffic source, and cart value, so the same generic popup doesn't hit every visitor
- Capture surfaces beyond popups, including banners, embedded forms, and slide-ins, let you mix mechanics on the same site
- A/B testing is built in, which matters when popup performance is your primary KPI

**Cons:**
- No transparent public starting price beyond the entry-tier ballpark; modular billing means evaluating real cost requires a sales conversation
- The rules engine has a real learning curve, so it pays off most for teams willing to spend time building targeting logic

**Verdict:** Pick Justuno when you want a popup and overlay program with serious targeting depth; skip it if a single welcome popup is the entire ask.

## 4. Octane AI

[Octane AI](https://octaneai.com) is a quiz-led product finder built primarily for Shopify, where the quiz itself is the hero discovery surface rather than open-ended chat or site search. For a marketing manager in beauty, skincare, or supplements, that focus is the point - and it's where the lead capture mechanic lives. Shoppers answer five to ten diagnostic questions about skin type, goals, or routine, and the platform maps answers to product attributes in the Shopify catalog to recommend a regimen. The email ask sits at the reveal step, so the quiz doubles as a high-completion capture surface with context attached. Octane ships pre-built templates for skincare routines, supplement regimens, and fit finders, plus a Klaviyo handoff that turns quiz answers into email-segment attributes lifecycle flows can act on.

**Best for:** Beauty, skincare, and CPG brands whose primary discovery surface is a diagnostic or routine-builder quiz funnel.

**Pricing:** Basic at $50/month (400 credits), Plus at $200/month (2,200 credits), Enterprise & Unlimited at $500+/month.

**Pros:**
- Quiz builder with deep Shopify product-attribute mapping, so recommendations and captured segment data tie back to real SKUs
- Pre-built templates for skincare routines, supplement regimens, and fit finders, which shortens time to launch
- Native Klaviyo integration captures quiz answers as email-segment attributes, so leads arrive in lifecycle email with context attached
- Lower engineering overhead than building quiz logic in-house on top of a headless storefront

**Cons:**
- Quiz-centric, so it is less effective for shoppers who prefer open-ended search or browsing over a scripted question tree
- Vertical fit narrows outside beauty and CPG, where scripted diagnostic questions don't map cleanly to catalog attributes

**Verdict:** Pick Octane AI when a quiz is genuinely the right discovery metaphor for your category; skip it if your catalog is too varied or technical for a scripted question tree to cover.

## Which lead capture tool fits your stack?

The right pick depends on where the capture moment lives in your funnel and how much you are willing to interrupt the shopper to get the email. Popup-first tools win on raw capture volume and install speed; quiz-led tools win on first-party data depth per lead. Match the tool to the mechanic you actually want to run, not to the one with the loudest demo.

Privy is the right call when the job is straightforward list growth on Shopify with popup mechanics. The install is fast, the entry tier starts at $30/month with 250 SMS credits, and email sends are unlimited. If your team needs an email capture surface live this week and you don't want to think about targeting rules for a while, Privy ships first and complains least.

Justuno earns its complexity when you want a single targeting engine running across popups, banners, overlays, and embedded forms with rules tied to visitor profiles. Pricing is modular and scales with campaign scope, which is fine if your team has the bandwidth to design and maintain the rule sets. Skip it if no one on staff owns conversion optimization day to day - the targeting depth goes to waste.

Octane AI is purpose-built for the quiz-as-discovery motion. If you sell skincare, supplements, or anything fit-driven where a guided diagnostic genuinely helps the shopper choose, the Basic tier at $50/month gets a quiz live with Klaviyo handoff. If your category doesn't naturally lend itself to a quiz, the format will feel forced and conversion will lag.

Nobi is the right pick when [better on-site search](https://nobi.ai/blog/best-ai-search-ecommerce-2026), a shopping assistant that answers real product questions, and automatic Q&A on shipping, sizing, and returns would move conversion more than adding another email capture surface. It is web-chat only and is not a popup or quiz tool, so brands whose actual need is email list growth will still want to pair Nobi with one of the three tools above.

## Frequently asked questions

Common questions marketing managers ask when picking between popup, quiz, and conversational tools for an ecommerce store.

**Do I need both a popup tool and a conversational tool, or can one cover lead capture end to end?**
Most teams run both. Privy or Justuno catches the email at first visit; Octane AI's quiz captures richer profile data deeper in the session. Nobi sits in the search and shopping-assistant lane rather than the email-capture lane, so pair it with a dedicated capture tool if list growth is the goal.

**How do these tools hand captured leads off to Klaviyo?**
Privy, Justuno, and Octane AI all push captured contacts and quiz answers into Klaviyo natively, so welcome flows and segment-based product recommendations fire within minutes.

**What is the realistic implementation time on Shopify?**
Privy and Justuno install as Shopify apps and ship in an afternoon. Octane AI takes longer because the quiz itself has to be designed and wired to your catalog - plan a week or two. Nobi installs through two JavaScript snippets in your theme, typically live in days.

**How do popup and quiz capture compare on lead quality versus raw volume?**
Popups maximize volume. Quizzes trade volume for richer first-party data per lead. Pick by which one your downstream lifecycle program is actually built to use.

**Which of these tools work outside of Shopify?**
Privy supports Shopify and BigCommerce. Justuno is platform-agnostic via a JavaScript snippet. Octane AI is Shopify-only. Nobi runs anywhere you can drop JavaScript: Shopify, BigCommerce, Magento, WooCommerce, and Salesforce Commerce Cloud.
